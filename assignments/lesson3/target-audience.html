Persona 1

Name: Kylo Renstrom

Age: 8

Profile: Kylo is studying weather this week in science class. He will be accessing the website with his parents through the family desktop.

Persona 2

Name: Danielle Jefferson

Age: 12

Profile: Danielle is enthusiastic about meteorology and is intrigued to learn more about her local climate. She will be accessing the website through her own laptop, and occasionally on a smart phone.

Scenario 1

Kylo's mother wants him to be appropriately dressed for school. With her help, he navigates to the page for his town, Soda Springs, and sees the day's forecast prominently displayed with a graphic showing what might be recommended for the weather.

Scenario 2

Kylo has an assignment which requires him to learn and understand the difference between several cloud types. Once on the home page, he can look through blog entries which will introduce topics such as climates, fronts, and different cloud types. Clicking on the article will bring him to a full page that will have a novice explanation for that topic.

Scenario 3

Danielle wants to learn more about the weather. She looks for the website's learning section and chooses an article from the blog that interests her.

Scenario 4

Danielle needs sources for a school project. From this website, she sees that local events are sourced from larger weather publications which she can use in conjunction with Lil Ol' Sun's visuals for her project.

Scenario 5

A teacher wants to display current weather-related events to her students. By clicking on her city's page and scrolling beneath the current weather information, she will be able to see articles covering what is happening around her.
