Hick's Law

The World's Worst Website Ever
https://theworldsworstwebsiteever.com/

This is the world's worst website. It was created to show as many awful beginner web design mistakes as possible. I chose this website to demonstrate how too many options for where your eyes to go or what to click on can freeze a user.

White Space

Audible
https://www.audible.com/

Audible's website shows a clean display, giving the visitor ample viewing space to move from one element to another, never cramming the viewport with more information than the visitor can digest in a short moment.

Contrast

Mobile Moxie
https://www.mobilemoxie.com/

Mobile Moxie's website shows good contrast in giving an interesting background color that meshes with their logo, text that can easily be seen over the background color, and a button with similarly good color contrast.
